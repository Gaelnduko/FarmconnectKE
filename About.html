FarmconnectKE

FarmconnectKE is a digital platform connecting local farmers directly with consumers and businesses, promoting fair trade, fresh produce, and sustainable agriculture in Kenya.

Core Idea

FarmConnectKE aims to bridge the gap between farmers who struggle to find reliable markets and consumers who want affordable, fresh produce. It’s a web-based marketplace that uses simple, accessible technology (HTML, CSS, JS, Tailwind) to make agricultural trade transparent, local, and efficient. Think of it as “Jumia for farm produce,” but focused on local farmers and community-driven growth
